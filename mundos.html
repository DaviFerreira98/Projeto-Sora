Twilight Town é um mundo que aparece em Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories, Kingdom Hearts II, Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days, e Kingdom Hearts III. É o "local de nascimento" de Roxas e desde então está infestado de Ninguém. No entanto, Heartless era conhecido por ter estado na cidade durante o tempo de Roxas na Organização.

Quadratum is a world that appeared in Kingdom Hearts III. Unlike other worlds, Quadratum is a world where it is located in another universe (also called "other side of reality") where the worlds are foreign to light and dark.

The Keyblade Graveyard is a world that appears in Kingdom Hearts II Final Mix, Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep, and Kingdom Hearts III. It was first revealed in the secret ending of Kingdom Hearts II, "The Gathering". Before its formal introduction as a world, the Keyblade Graveyard was known as "The Badlands" (謎の荒野 Nazo no Arano?, lit. "The Mysterious Wasteland").

Toy Box is a world in the game Kingdom Hearts III. It is based on Disney/Pixar's Toy Story series. Andy and some of the toys have gone missing. Gameplay takes place in several notable areas from Toy Story, including Andy's Bedroom, and the yard outside Andy's house.

Monstropolis is a world in the game, Kingdom Hearts III. It is based on Disney Pixar's 2001 film Monsters, Inc. In this world, Sora, Donald, and Goofy become monsters resembling those of Monstropolis' citizens. When using Formchange, Sora's fur will change color depending on the one being used.

The Caribbean is a world in the game, Kingdom Hearts III. It is based on Disney's film franchise Pirates of the Caribbean, originally published in 2003. Unlike the last time they visited, Sora, Donald, and Goofy alter their appearance to fit better with the aesthetics of this world, looking more realistic and detailed than usual. They also trade in their usual gear for pirate clothes, with Sora being the captain.
